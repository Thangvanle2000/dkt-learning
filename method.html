Introduce

Natural learning Balancing method

AVOS helps learners discover their own principles without focusing on the rules of the English - similar to when a child is just learning to speak his or her mother tongue. This method is called "natural learning", which is very suitable for developing a solid foundation of the language as well as the rules of that language.

Communication tool Real-life communication

English is a key communication tool. AVOS takes a practical approach by focusing on content where learners really want to apply the language in their lives.
Learners will develop the vocabulary and grammar needed for this communication through a variety of reading.
